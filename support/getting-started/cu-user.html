Users with an existing CU-Boulder or CU-Denver account can request a Research Computing account using the RC Account Request portal, and will authenticate to the RC environment using a Digipass one-time password authenticator.

Complete the account request form

Visit the RC Account Request portal to complete the account reqeust form. You will first be prompted to select your affiliation: "University of Colorado - Boulder" or "University of Colorado - Denver." Select the appropriate affiliation to continue.

If you are a CU-Boulder user, you will then be prompted to authenticate using your CU IdentiKey credentials (i.e., your username and password). If you are a CU-Denver user, you will be prompted to fill out a more general-purpose identification form. In either case, submit your request. After review, your account will be created by a member of the Research Computing staff, and login details will be provided to you by email.

Get and register an authenticator

Access to Research Computing resources requires two-factor authentication. Request your physical one-time-password (OTP) generator device by either

  • filling out the OIT Authenticator order form, or
  • emailing your request to rc-help@colorado.edu.

Be prepared to show your BuffOne card or other state-issued ID when you pick up your authenticator.
