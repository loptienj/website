Affiliates of NCAR have access to the Janus compute cluster through CISL.

Apply for an allocation

CU-Boulder Research Computing accounts for NCAR users are created as part of the CISL allocation process. Submit a request for an allocation on the Janus cluster using the CISL allocation request process. Once your request is approved, you will receive login details, including your RC username.

When you log into RC systems, authenticate to your RC account using your NCAR Yubikey or CryptoCard. (More information on logging into RC systems is available in the user guide, and later in the getting started guide.

Next steps

Once you have received your Janus allocation and RC account, continue getting started with the shared computing infrastructure.
